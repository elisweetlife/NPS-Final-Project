Double OLO Line Build

This is a double sided OLO line specific to the Mountain View, CA location. This line moves opposite of the single OLO line, from right to left instead. Starting with COLD Green Bases. After the cold bases, there is a table top tray for 3 HOT Bases. Dressings, Toppings 1 follow the bases. After Toppings 1 there is a lower hot drawer for all the hot held ingredients. Next is Toppings-2 with Crunches located above Toppings 2. Finish off with the breads.
