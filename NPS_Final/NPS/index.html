Mountain View Homebase

Mountain View is a city in Santa Clara County, California, United States, in Silicon Valley. Named for its views of the Santa Cruz Mountains, Mountain View has developed from its origins as a stagecoach stop into one of the principal cities of Silicon Valley. It has a population of 74,066.

The Mountain View Sweetgreen is 1 of the 8 bay area restaurants. It is equiped with a Front of House line, single OLO line, and coming soon a double OLO line in the "Closet" to support Google and Facebook.
